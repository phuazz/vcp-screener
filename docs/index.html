How to read this. Candidates have passed the eight Trend Template conditions, an RS-rank floor, a liquidity floor, and the full VCP test (tightening contractions, dry volume, a tight final contraction). The pivot is the breakout line; the stop sits below the last contraction low; risk is the pivot-to-stop distance. The watchlist holds names in confirmed uptrends whose base is not yet clean — the tags show what is missing.

Caveats. Prototype data is from yfinance (no delisted history, best for liquid large caps) over a curated Russell 1000 seed, not the full index. This is a research signal, not investment advice, and not a recommendation to trade any security. Method and limitations: github.com/phuazz/vcp-screener.
